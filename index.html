We ran away and got
married!

That's right!

The two of us have always liked to do things a little differently. So it only seemed natural that we wouldn't have a traditional engagement and wedding, either.

For us, the important part of getting married wasn't the proposal, a ring, a dress, or flowers. Instead, we wanted to have a wedding where we could fully focus on each other, with no audience or distractions.

We were married on September 17, 2015 in a private ceremony at City Hall.
